5 Common Diseases you may suffer during Winter Season


Disease:Pneumonia


Pneumonia is a life-threatening disease in which viral and bacterial infections spread out in the alveoli.


PRECAUTIONS:

1. the PCV13 (Prevnar 13) and PPSV23 (Pneumovax) shots may be taken
2. getting proper exercise, rest and diet to lower the risks of getting bacterial pneumonia.
3. In cases of severe or stubborn pneumonia, oxygen treatment should be taken.

a)pulmonologist

Disease: Norovirus


The norovirus is an infectious illness that can occur to people of all ages and at any time of the year. However, it is more common in the winter.


PRECAUTIONS:

1.Following proper hand and oral hygiene.
2.Eating thoroughly cooked food. 3.Cleaning and disinfecting surfaces in the home.


CONSULT:

a)Gastroenterologist

Disease: Strep Throat


Strep throat, caused by group A Streptococcus bacteria, is a critical infection occurring in the throat and tonsils. It is a common illness caused by cold weather in children within the age group of 5 to 15 years.


PRECAUTIONS:

1. Drink more warm fluids during the season. 2.Stay hydrated.
3.Wash your hands frequently.


CONSULT:

a)ENT specialist

Disease: Influenza


Influenza (flu) is a viral disease that causes widespread illness every year. Immunisation and practising prevention measures for people who are at risk of complications from the flu are the best ways we can reduce the number of influenza infections and deaths.


PRECAUTIONS:

1.administration of influenza vaccine
2.implementation of respiratory hygiene and cough etiquette
3.appropriate management of ill HCP


CONSULT:

a) pulmonologist

Disease: Bronchiolitis


Viruses that cause bronchiolitis spread easily through the air when someone with an infection coughs or sneezes. Germs can stay on hands, toys, doorknobs, tissues, and other surfaces.


PRECAUTIONS:

1) Avoid smoking around the child
2)Wash hands frequently with soap and water, especially before touching an infant.
