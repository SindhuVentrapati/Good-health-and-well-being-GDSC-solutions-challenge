5 Common Diseases you may suffer during Rainy Season


Disease: Leptospirosis


This is a bacterial disease that affects both humans and animals. In humans, it can be caused when people come into contact with soil, water, or food contaminated with urine, blood, or tissue of the infected animal.


PRECAUTIONS:

1.Avoid a puddle on a street since it may be contaminated by animal urine
2.wear water repellent boots


CONSULT:

a)Healthcare Provider

Disease: Diarrhea


This disease often occurs during rainy season as food or water maybe contaminated, contributing to the infection in the digestive system. The symptom of diarrhea is watery, liquid stools. Other symptoms may include fever or stomach cramps. If there is blood in stools, you may assume that you have developed dysentery.


PRECAUTIONS:

Have thoroughly cooked, and steaming-hot food, always use serving spoons, wash hands frequently, and never use other people's cutlery or utensils.


CONSULT:

a)gastroenterologist

Disease: Dengue Fever


This disease is caused by Aedes mosquitoes which love water and humidity. Besides, the disease can be found with encephalitis and malaria. The first stage of the symptoms can include 2-7 days of a fever and muscle aches.


PRECAUTIONS:

Avoid the areas full of mosquitoes, use a mosquito repellent, sleep inside a mosquito net, don’t take aspirin since it may cause bleeding disorder which is very harmful.


CONSULT:

a)General Surgeon b)Medical Officer

Disease: FLU


This disease is caused by a virus through respiratory system. A common fever is normal but when it comes to a flu during a rainy season, the complication such as pneumonia may occur.


PRECAUTIONS:

Wash hands every time you go to a toilet or arrive home, do not stay in confined area, and wear a mask whenever you have weakened immunity.


CONSULT:

General Surgeon

Disease: Hand-Foot-And-Mouth Disease


This disease is a contagious viral infection. The illness spreads by person-to-person contact with infected person’s nasal secretions, saliva, and stool.


PRECAUTIONS

Have thoroughly cooked, and steaming-hot food, don't use other people's items, wash hands once in contact with nasal secretions, saliva and immediately wash poop-stained clothes.


CONSULT:

a)General Surgeon b)Dermatologist
